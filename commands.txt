// Starter commands
init <name> - Configure your shareBox number.
setup <boxName> - Create a box to share with your friends.
join <code> - Join a box using the provided unique short code.

// Box commands
<amount> - The amount to be split amongst all sharers in your box.
<ower> <amount> - The amount the ower has to spend you.
<payer> <ower> <amount> - The amount the ower has to send the payer.
